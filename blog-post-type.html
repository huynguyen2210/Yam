Who can become a YamCreator?

Who can become a YamCreator? Breeze through the process with Yam!
21.06.2021 · Author

Dám /jæm/:

Vietnamese kids are all very talented. However, growing up their passions are often lost or manipulated by traditional values and social norms. Exposure to new technologies and job opportunities has not made it easier for them. More than often they have no other choice but to compromise, to give up, to find a safe zone to operate in instead of following their passion and making a living out of it.

In society nowadays, to stay in a comfort zone means to fall behind. One has to push, to experience, to innovate, daring to fail in order to succeed.

That is why Yam (tone similar to Dám - Dare in Vietnamese) wants to embrace the new Vietnamese generations, accompany and motivate them to live up to their own passion, help them:

Dare to think
Dare to do
Dare to create their own legacy.

To provide an exclusive, transparent, ad-free space where Vietnamese content creators can freely create, connect, and monetize on their passions.
