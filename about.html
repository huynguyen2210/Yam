About Yam

A passionate playground for content creators & fans.

Dám /jæm/:

Dare to think
Dare to do
Dare to create their own legacy.

Who are we?

YAM is a streaming & live entertainment mobile app for content creator & fans. We are the very first Viet-made subscription based social platform, designed for creators to connect with fans. An interactive & empowering playground with exclusive content only on YAM. An exciting home for new & potential influencers, KOLs to express your talent & passion.

Our vision

Embrace Vietnamese creativity and be the guardian for passion in creativity.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide an exclusive, transparent, ad-free space where Vietnamese content creators can freely create, connect, and monetize on their passions.
