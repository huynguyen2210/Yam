About Yam

A passionate playground for content creators & fans.

Dám /jæm/:

Dare to think
Dare to do
Dare to create their own legacy.

Who are we?

YAM is a streaming & live entertainment mobile app for content creator & fans. We are the very first Viet-made subscription based social platform, designed for creators to connect with fans. An interactive & empowering playground with exclusive content only on YAM. An exciting home for new & potential influencers, KOLs to express your talent & passion.

Our vision

Embrace Vietnamese creativity and be the guardian for passion in creativity.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide an exclusive, transparent, ad-free space where Vietnamese content creators can freely create, connect, and monetize on their passions.

Our Promise

Being a friend of creative Millennials and Gen Zs, Yam shares the same personality to best get along with each individual: young, dynamic and passionate, and patriotic - one significant factor to prove Yam always prioritizes the Vietnamese creative community. As our core values, Yam promises to always bring:
Transparency
Yam never hides. Stakeholders will always know exactly what they get from Yam.
Meaningful Connection
Exclusive content, personal interaction for fans, and sustainable income for creators. The bond on Yam is through quality content, not views.
Freedom
Creativity has no borders. Creators on Yam have complete freedom to create artistic yet lawful work, express their point of view and earn money from their passion.
Authenticity
Embrace authentic creativity, embrace real perspective and embrace original artwork of talented Vietnamese.

We are a part of
ME Group Asia.

With the mission of being the most reliable and forward-thinking production hub in Vietnam for global creations, ME Group thrives on existing as a sustainable ecosystem of independent production teams, offering two of the most critical assets in this industry: agility & expertise.
